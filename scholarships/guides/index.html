Guide Application

Think about your first conference. What do you wish someone had told you? What do you wish someone had showed you? At Keep Ruby Weird, we offer an Opportunity Scholarship to those that would not be able to attend a conference otherwise. We pair scholarship recipients with guides to show them around.

As a guide it is your goal to make your attendee's conference experience a better one. You will be tasked with being a resource to your assigned scholarship recipient. You will be expected to remain available for their questions and to help them connect to others at the conference.

Being a guide is a great excuse to go talk to speakers and introduce them to your attendee. It's a great way to meet other guides and other conference attendees. Make a difference in someones life today, apply to be a guide today.

For inspiration you may want to talk about:

  • What makes a good conference experience for you
  • Why you want to be a guide
  • Be creative
