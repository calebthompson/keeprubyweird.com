Opportunity Scholarship Application

Keep Ruby Weird believes in diversity. To support this goal we are proud to offer an Opportunity Scholarship. This scholarship goes to individuals who may not be be able to attend a tech conference otherwise.

The opportunity scholarship recipients will receive free admission to all conference events as well as a guide to help them navigate the conference. We are currently unable to provide any assistance with transportation or lodging.

If you are interested in applying for this award, please fill out the below form. If you have any questions, please email organizers@keeprubyweird.com.
